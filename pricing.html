Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our pricing plans

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for more information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual subscriptions.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans. You only pay the monthly or annual subscription fee.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. Contact support if you're not satisfied.
